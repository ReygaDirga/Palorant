MAPS

ABIS

Abis is one of the large underground maps, where this area reflects a dark and challenging atmosphere, creating a thrilling adventure sensation in the underground world. Complete with dramatic lighting and realistic shadow effects, Abis is the perfect backdrop for intense combat or exploration.

Blind

Blind has become a fan favorite among the global community. With two spike locations, a one-way teleporter, and no Middle area to fight in, Blind is a map that guarantees challenging and dynamic competition between opponents that is full of surprises for both attackers and defenders.

HaveFun

HaveFun Map is one of the maps that has three main areas, namely A Site, B Site, and C Site, as well as several corridors or roads that connect the three areas. On the HaveFun Map, the attacker or defender team must choose one of the sites that will be targeted to plant or prevent spikes.

Peral

Peral is a unique map set in a magnificent and colorful underwater city. Featuring a futuristic city setting on Omega Earth, Peral is the first map to truly focus on geographic design without any additional complex mechanics. The city combines the beauty of underwater architecture with dynamic gameplay experience.

Splitz

Splitz is a pair of locations split by a higher center that allows for quick movement using two ropes to ascend. Each location is built with a towering tower that is vital for control. Don't forget to watch the top side before it's too late.

Sanset

Sanset is one of Palorant’s more traditional maps with two locations and three lanes. One additional feature is a mechanical door found between B Market and Mid Courtyard. Players can use the switch on the Market side of the door to close or open it.

Ascend

The map has a classic Italian architectural theme, with lots of corners and narrow streets making it ideal for tactical play and close combat. The open center area is key on this map, as controlling this area can give you a huge advantage in capturing both bomb sites.
